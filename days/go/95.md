# Day 95: Mentoring and Team Practices Project

## 📝 Today's Tasks

1.  **Pair on a Topic:** Teach a junior-friendly topic (errors, ctx, or testing) in writing.
2.  **Improve Onboarding:** Add a CONTRIBUTING or onboarding section for your project.
3.  **Review Simulation:** Review an older commit of yours with fresh eyes and note fixes.
4.  **Knowledge Share:** Create a short cheat-sheet for your team's Go conventions.

## 📖 Task Explanations

Senior impact includes multiplying others. Documentation and mentoring habits scale teams.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

Stronger team practices: onboarding docs, conventions, and reflective review.

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **Onboarding:** The process that helps a new contributor become productive.
*   **Convention:** An agreed team practice beyond what the language requires.
*   **Multiplier Effect:** Impact gained by helping others move faster.
