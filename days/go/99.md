# Day 99: Ultimate Capstone Planning

## 📝 Today's Tasks

1.  **Define Ambition:** Scope an enterprise-grade service using most of your toolkit.
2.  **Non-Functionals:** List SLOs, security, observability, and deploy requirements.
3.  **Work Breakdown:** Plan implementation chunks for Day 100 and follow-up polish.
4.  **Risk List:** Identify top risks (scope, unfamiliar tech) and mitigations.

## 📖 Task Explanations

Great capstones are scoped. Non-functional requirements separate demos from professional systems.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

A realistic ultimate-capstone plan with NFRs, milestones, and risks.

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **NFR:** Non-functional requirement such as latency, security, or availability.
*   **Risk Mitigation:** An action that reduces the likelihood or impact of a risk.
*   **Work Breakdown:** Splitting a large goal into executable tasks.
