# Day 69: Retries, Idempotency, Circuit Breakers

## 📝 Today's Tasks

1.  **Retry Budgets:** Retry transient failures with exponential backoff + jitter.
2.  **Idempotency Keys:** Design safe retries for writes.
3.  **Circuit Breaker:** Trip after repeated failures to a dependency.
4.  **Don't Amplify:** Avoid retry storms.

## 📖 Task Explanations

Resilience patterns protect users during partial outages. Retries without idempotency and budgets cause outages.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

Safer retry policies and a basic circuit-breaker awareness in clients.

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **Exponential Backoff:** Increasing wait time between retries.
*   **Jitter:** Randomness added to backoff to avoid synchronized retries.
*   **Circuit Breaker:** A pattern that temporarily stops calling a failing dependency.
