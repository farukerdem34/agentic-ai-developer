# Day 49: Authorization and RBAC

## 📝 Today's Tasks

1.  **Roles/Permissions:** Model roles or permissions for resources.
2.  **Authorize in Handlers/Services:** Enforce checks after authentication.
3.  **Deny by Default:** Missing permission means 403.
4.  **Tests:** Cover allowed and forbidden paths.

## 📖 Task Explanations

Authentication identifies who; authorization decides what they can do. Keep checks close to business rules and default-deny.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

Role- or permission-based authorization with tested deny/allow paths.

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **RBAC:** Role-Based Access Control.
*   **403 Forbidden:** HTTP status when authenticated but not permitted.
*   **Least Privilege:** Granting only the permissions needed.
