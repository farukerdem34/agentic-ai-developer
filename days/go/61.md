# Day 61: Pipelines and Fan-Out Patterns

## 📝 Today's Tasks

1.  **Stage Functions:** Build a multi-stage pipeline with channels.
2.  **Cancellation:** Thread context through stages so they exit.
3.  **Backpressure:** Observe what happens when consumers are slow.
4.  **Cleanup:** Ensure all stages terminate without leaks.

## 📖 Task Explanations

Pipelines express streaming transformations concurrently. Cancellation and teardown are the hard parts—treat them as first-class requirements.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

Robust pipeline stages that respect cancellation and avoid leaks.

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **Pipeline:** A series of concurrent stages connected by channels.
*   **Backpressure:** Slowdown propagating upstream when consumers can't keep up.
*   **Teardown:** Ordered shutdown of concurrent components.
