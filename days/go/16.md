# Day 16: Goroutines

## 📝 Today's Tasks

1.  **Spawn Goroutines:** Launch concurrent work with the `go` keyword.
2.  **Wait for Completion:** Use `sync.WaitGroup` to wait for goroutines.
3.  **Avoid Orphans:** Ensure goroutines exit; discuss leaks briefly.
4.  **Concurrent Fan-out:** Process a list of jobs concurrently and collect results carefully.

## 📖 Task Explanations

Goroutines are lightweight concurrent functions. Without synchronization, results are lost or races occur. WaitGroups coordinate completion; later days add channels for communication.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

You will start goroutines safely and wait for them without races or premature exit.

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **Goroutine:** A lightweight concurrent execution managed by the Go runtime.
*   **`sync.WaitGroup`:** A counter used to wait for a collection of goroutines to finish.
*   **Goroutine Leak:** A goroutine that never exits, wasting resources.
