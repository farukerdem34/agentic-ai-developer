# Day 94: ADRs and Technical Communication

## 📝 Today's Tasks

1.  **Write an ADR:** Document a significant decision (router, DB, auth model).
2.  **Options Considered:** Include trade-offs and why rejected alternatives lost.
3.  **Status:** Mark accepted/superseded as decisions evolve.
4.  **Shareable Summary:** Practice explaining the decision in 5 sentences.

## 📖 Task Explanations

Architecture Decision Records capture why—not just what. They prevent teams from relitigating the past.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

A clear ADR and the habit of explaining trade-offs succinctly.

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **ADR:** Architecture Decision Record.
*   **Trade-off:** A balance between competing qualities such as simplicity vs flexibility.
*   **Superseded:** An ADR status meaning a later decision replaced it.
