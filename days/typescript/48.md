# Day 48: Bundlers, tsc Emit, and Transpilation Split

## 📝 Today's Tasks

1.  **Compare Modes:** Type-check with `tsc --noEmit`; transpile with esbuild/swc/vite.
2.  **isolatedModules:** Enable and fix incompatibilities.
3.  **Source Maps:** Generate maps for debugging.
4.  **Choose a Pipeline:** Document whether tsc emits JS or only type-checks in your project.

## 📖 Task Explanations

Modern pipelines often split typechecking from emitting. `isolatedModules` keeps single-file transpilers happy. Be explicit about who emits JS.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

A clear typecheck-vs-emit pipeline appropriate for your tooling.

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **`noEmit`:** Type-check without writing JS files.
*   **`isolatedModules`:** Restricts features that need whole-program emit.
*   **Transpiler:** A tool that converts TS/JS syntax without full typechecking.
